üî¨ Core Concepts & Theory
1. Technical Architecture
The system is built on a four-pillar technical framework:


Data Source Integration: Centralizing data from diverse supply chain databases and systems.


Data Processing: Rigorous cleaning, handling missing values, and transforming data to ensure consistency for analysis.


Qlik Sense Platform: Utilizing the platform's associative engine for robust data visualization and dashboard creation.


User Interface: Developing interactive, responsive dashboards to facilitate real-time decision-making.

2. Business Intelligence Strategy
The project addresses critical business challenges by providing:


Enhanced Visibility: Real-time tracking and monitoring of supply chain processes.


Operational Efficiency: Optimizing transportation routes through historical logistics data analysis to reduce operational costs.


Market Responsiveness: Improving the ability to meet fluctuating market changes and customer demands.

üìä Data Visualization & Analysis
A comprehensive suite of visualizations was developed to analyze performance:


Regional Demand Analysis: Bar charts displaying total items placed by customer country and state.


Customer Segmentation: Pie charts illustrating the distribution of different customer segments.


Financial Performance: Line charts and scatter plots analyzing "Benefit per Order" trends and profit ratios.


Market Logistics: Heat maps and map visualizations showing sales distribution and order regions.
